# Journey
Journey is a narrative- & exploration-driven miniRPG: explore a map, fight against enemies and get to know NPCs and the character you've chosen.

## Overview
V0.1 is a short prototype with tutorial.
Currently included:
- choose your class
- visit the shop
- (steal the weird potion)
- talk to the tavernkeeper
- fight against the rat in their basement <br>
After that, the prototype ends. You can choose to look at the map and fight against the 3 hidden enemies or look for the hidden camp, which will magically heal you, but starting here everything is still WIP.

## Assignment
Standalone 2D or 3D Game with Clear Core Mechanics <br>
<i>(Gameplay & Mechanics Focus)</i> <br>

<b>Project Idea</b><br>
Development of a standalone game that focuses on clearly defined core mechanics.<br>
<br>
Focus:<br>
• Clarity<br>
• Gameplay feel<br>
• Clean gameplay loop<br>
<i>Not on scope, but on quality.</i><br>

<b>Goals</b><br>

## Controls
• WASD or arrows to move<br>
  (mind the hexagonal tile map: clicking left/right slightly before up/down is recommended)<br>
• space or leftclick to submit<br>
• ESC for pausemenu (only after tutorial)<br>

## Features
- Hexagon Tile Map
- inventory
- choose between 3 classes
- class-specific dialogue (all classes) and opportunity (thief only)
- 3 enemies (all rats)
- simple fight-system
